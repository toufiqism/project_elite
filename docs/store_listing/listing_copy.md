## Full description (4000 chars max)
Project Elite is a self-improvement system, not another to-do app. It pulls study, prayer, fitness, and habit tracking into one disciplined daily loop — and grades you against your own goals.

**Built for people who want output, not motivation.**

▸ **Study tracker** — log focused sessions by subject (including full CA Certificate, Professional, and Advance subject lists for Pakistan CA students). See weekly hours, focus depth, and which subjects you've been avoiding.

▸ **Habit system** — set daily habits, see streaks, never lose a day to "I forgot." Composite-key storage means streak math is instant even after months of data.

▸ **Prayer times + Qibla** — accurate prayer times via AlAdhan API with manual overrides, plus a compass-based Qibla finder, Hijri date, dua collection, and tasbih counter. Karachi calculation method + Hanafi madhab defaults.

▸ **Fitness** — log workouts and weight, search a 1000+ exercise database, watch how-to videos.

▸ **Ayanokoji Mode** — track discipline stats inspired by *Classroom of the Elite*. Every focused session, completed prayer, kept habit feeds your character. Title ranks (Mastermind, Elite, etc.) reflect real consistency.

▸ **Dashboard** — one-number daily score (40% study + 35% habits + 25% prayer) so you know if today was real progress or just busywork.

▸ **Reliable reminders** — multi-day notification scheduling, exact alarms, OEM-specific battery-killer guidance for Xiaomi, Vivo, OPPO, Huawei, Samsung. Reminders fire even if the app is closed.

**Privacy by design:**
- Local-first storage. Your data lives on your phone in encrypted Hive boxes.
- Optional Google sign-in adds cloud backup (Firestore) — your document, your data, only you can read it.
- Zero ads. Zero trackers. Zero analytics SDKs.
- In-app account deletion permanently erases cloud + local data.

**Permissions:**
- Location → prayer times + Qibla (never shared)
- Notifications + exact alarms → prayer adhan + reminders at the correct second
- Boot completed → re-arm reminders after restart
- Battery optimization exemption → so OEM battery savers don't kill prayer alarms

Project Elite is for students, professionals, and anyone serious about turning daily intent into long-term identity. No streak-shaming, no manipulative gamification — just clean tools and an honest score.
